What are we researching?

  1. Which product categories and subcategories drive the most sales and profit?
  2. How do sales vary by region and state?
  3. Are there seasonal or monthly sales fluctuations?
  4. Which customer segments generate the most revenue?
  5. How do discounts affect profit margins?

  2. Regional Trends: The West region and California lead in total sales.

  5. Discount Strategy: Moderate discounts improve sales; excessive ones reduce profit.
